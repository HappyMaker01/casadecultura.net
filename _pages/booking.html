The Twin Room

This room features two beds and wooden frame windows overlooking the orchard. You can close the window blinds if you don't want to be woken up by the natural light.

The bathroom and the kitchen are shared with the double room.

The Double Room

This room features one big bed and wooden frame windows overlooking the orchard and the yard. You can close the window blinds if you don't want to be woken up by the natural light.

The bathroom and the kitchen are shared with the twin room.

Shared spaces

The two rooms share a fully equipped kitchen (including gas stove and electric oven), a living room and a bathroom.
